Dr. Mohammed Sameerudeen

MBBS, DO, DNB

Chief ophthalmologist at Indiana hospital Mangalore. He completed his MBBS from Tumkur, DO from KMC Mangalore, and DNB from Fr Mullins. Specialized in cataract, refractive and glaucoma surgeries. Dr. Prashanthkumar Achar
